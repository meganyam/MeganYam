Founded in 2000 by Lorraine Zander, Faze is a print and online publication that targets the demographic of millenial women. From entertainment to travel, Faze aims to empower young women with uplifting news on a diverse range of current topics. As an editorial contributor, I have covered multiple stories in both print and online involving technology, movie reviews, lifestyle and more.
